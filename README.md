# Cryptid Clash: Echoes of the Unknown

![Godot Engine](https://img.shields.io/badge/Godot-4.x-%23478CBF?logo=godot-engine&logoColor=white)

**A feature-rich match-3 puzzle game with a cryptid hunting theme.**

Hunt legendary creatures like Bigfoot, Mothman, Nessie, Chupacabra, and more in this addictive puzzle adventure. Build your dream team of cryptids, master powerful abilities, and unravel mysteries across different regions.

## Features

- **Classic yet deep Match-3 gameplay** on an 8×8 grid
- **Cryptid Team System** — Collect and upgrade powerful creatures with unique leader skills and abilities
- **Mana & Ability System** — Charge mana through matches and unleash devastating special moves
- **Progressive Obstacles** — Ice blocks, spider webs, and more that evolve as you advance
- **Cascading Combos** with scoring multipliers and booster creation
- **Rich Progression** — Regions, levels, stars, credibility XP, and energy system
- **Live-ops Features** — Daily login, Field Pass (battle pass), gacha with pity system, shop
- **Field Guide** — Collect and learn about all cryptids you discover
- **Polished Mobile Experience** — Smooth touch controls, screen shake, particle effects, and satisfying audio
